# Bill of Materials (BOM)

## Tier 1: MVP Baseline

- Global shutter camera (640x480 is acceptable for MVP)
- Fixed lens (low distortion preferred)
- Neutral LED lighting strips/panels for under-table illumination
- Acrylic plate (transparent)
- Grid fixture layer (laser-cut or 3D printed template)
- Frame structure (aluminum profile or rigid wood)
- Black matte shielding material
- PC workstation (mid-range CPU, 16GB+ RAM)
- Single projector (1080p, suitable brightness for room conditions)

## Tier 2: Stability Upgrade

- Higher resolution global shutter camera
- Better lens and calibration target kit
- Diffuse panel for more uniform lighting
- Service hatch and cable management accessories
- UPS for graceful shutdown and calibration safety

## Tier 3: Showcase Upgrade

- Higher brightness projector
- Secondary display for metrics/operator panel
- Optional second camera for redundancy
- Better table finish and enclosure design

## Consumables and Fabrication

- Mount brackets, screws, standoffs
- Cable ties, adhesive clips
- Calibration print sheets
- Lego-compatible base plates by class usage
- Spare block bases and labels

## Procurement Notes

- Do not optimize for max grid size first.
- Prioritize calibration stability and maintenance access.
- Keep replaceable parts easy to source locally.
